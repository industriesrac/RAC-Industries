The Founders

The journey of RAC Industries began with three passionate minds — Readouan Hridoy, Masum Hossain, and Mottakinul Islam.

Together, they shared one common dream: to bring back the purity, authenticity, and pride of Bangladeshi spices.

Founded in 2023 in Bogura, Bangladesh, RAC Industries was built on their dedication to producing 100% pure, hygienically processed, and quality-assured food products under the trusted brand name Mezban.
