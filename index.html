About RAC Industries

RAC Industries is a trusted food processing company from Bogura, Bangladesh, delivering premium quality food products under the Mezban brand.

We focus on purity, freshness, and quality assurance by carefully selecting ingredients and maintaining hygienic processing standards. Our mission is to provide safe, delicious, and reliable food products for families.

The Founders

The journey of RAC Industries began with three passionate minds — Readouan Hridoy, Masum Hossain, and Mottakinul Islam.

United by a shared vision, they dreamed of bringing back the purity, authenticity, and pride of Bangladeshi spices to every household.

Founded in 2023 in Bogura, Bangladesh, RAC Industries was built on their commitment to producing 100% pure, hygienically processed, and quality-assured food products under the trusted brand name Mezban.

With dedication, innovation, and a promise of quality, the founders continue to build a brand that represents trust, tradition, and authentic taste.
