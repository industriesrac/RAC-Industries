The Founders

The success story of RAC Industries began with three visionary minds — Readouan Hridoy, Masum Hossain, and Mottakinul Islam.

Together, they shared one common dream: to bring back the purity, authenticity, and pride of Bangladeshi spices.

Founded in 2023 in Bogura, Bangladesh, RAC Industries was established with a commitment to producing 100% pure, hygienically processed, and premium-quality food products under the trusted MEZBAN brand.

Today, the company continues to grow with a strong focus on quality, innovation, customer satisfaction, and long-term trust.

Our Vision

To become one of Bangladesh's most trusted food brands by delivering safe, pure, and premium-quality products while maintaining the highest standards of quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction.

Our Mission

  • ✅ Deliver premium-quality food products.
  • ✅ Maintain strict hygiene and quality control.
  • ✅ Use carefully selected raw materials.
  • ✅ Build long-term trust with customers and business partners.
  • ✅ Represent Bangladesh with pride through the MEZBAN brand.

Let's Build Trust Together

At RAC Industries, we believe that quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction are the foundations of a successful business. Every MEZBAN product reflects our commitment to excellence and our promise to deliver pure, safe, and premium-quality food products.
