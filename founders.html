The Founders

The success story of RAC Industries began with three visionary minds — Readouan Hridoy, Masum Hossain, and Mottakinul Islam.

Together, they shared one common dream: to bring back the purity, authenticity, and pride of Bangladeshi spices.

Founded in 2023 in Bogura, Bangladesh, RAC Industries was born out of their dedication to producing 100% pure, chemical-free, and hygienically processed spices under the trusted brand name Mezban.

Our Vision

The founders of RAC Industries believe that quality food begins with honesty, purity, and dedication.

Their vision is to build a trusted Bangladeshi food brand that delivers authentic taste, safe products, and consistent quality to every family.

Through the Mezban brand, RAC Industries continues its journey to preserve traditional flavors while maintaining modern hygiene and quality standards.
