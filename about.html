About RAC Industries

RAC Industries is a trusted food processing company from Bogura, Bangladesh, delivering premium quality food products under the trusted brand name Mezban.

We are committed to producing pure, hygienic, and high-quality food products by carefully selecting raw materials and maintaining strict processing standards.

Our product range includes premium quality spices, mustard oil, aromatic rice, and other essential food products designed to bring authentic taste and confidence to every household.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide safe, pure, and reliable food products while preserving the traditional taste of Bangladeshi cuisine.

We believe that quality, honesty, and customer satisfaction are the foundation of a successful food brand.

Our Values

  • ✅ Purity & Authenticity
  • ✅ Quality Food Production
  • ✅ Customer Trust
  • ✅ Hygienic Processing Standards
  • ✅ Continuous Improvement

Our Commitment

At RAC Industries, we are continuously working to improve our products, maintain quality standards, and deliver the authentic taste that customers can trust.

Under the Mezban brand, our goal is to become a trusted Bangladeshi food brand known for purity, quality, and customer satisfaction.
