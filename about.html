Who We Are

RAC Industries is a proudly Bangladeshi food processing company founded in 2023 in Bogura, Bangladesh.

Under our trusted MEZBAN brand, we produce premium-quality spices, mustard oil, and aromatic rice using carefully selected raw materials and hygienic processing methods.

Our goal is simple—to provide pure, safe and authentic food products that families can trust every day.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide premium-quality food products that meet the highest standards of purity, hygiene, and taste. We are committed to delivering safe and authentic products that add value to every family's kitchen.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become one of Bangladesh's most trusted food brands by consistently delivering quality products, embracing innovation, and building lasting relationships with customers, distributors, and business partners.

Why RAC Industries?

At RAC Industries, we believe that quality is not just a promise—it is our responsibility. From sourcing carefully selected ingredients to hygienic processing and secure packaging, every step reflects our commitment to excellence.

Through our trusted MEZBAN brand, we continue to serve families with safe, pure, and premium-quality food products while building long-term trust with customers and business partners.
